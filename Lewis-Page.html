After six successful years with McLaren, Lewis Hamilton made the bold decision to join Mercedes AMG Petronas F1 Team for the 2013 season. Many questioned this move, as Mercedes had yet to establish themselves as championship contenders.

2013 Season Highlights

The Hungarian Grand Prix proved to be the turning point. Starting from pole position, Lewis delivered a masterclass in wet weather driving, securing his first victory for Mercedes and silencing the doubters.

"I knew this was the right move. Mercedes had the vision, the resources, and the determination to build something special. This is just the beginning." — Lewis Hamilton

The 2021 season was one of the most intense and controversial in Formula 1 history. Lewis Hamilton faced a fierce challenge from Red Bull's Max Verstappen, with the championship battle going down to the final race.

2021 Season Highlights

After securing his 100th Grand Prix victory in Russia, Lewis entered the season finale at Yas Marina with a chance to claim an unprecedented 8th World Title. In a dramatic final lap under controversial safety car procedures, Max Verstappen overtook him on fresher tires to win the race — and the championship — by a single point.

"It's not how I wanted it to end… but I'm proud of what we built. We gave it everything we had." — Lewis Hamilton
